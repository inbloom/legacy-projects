students
URI Structure:
https://example.com/api/rest/v1.0/students

This entity represents an individual for whom instruction, services and/or care are provided in an early childhood, elementary or secondary educational program under the jurisdiction of a school, education agency, or other institution or program. A student is a person who has been enrolled in a school or other educational institution. For more information, see the schema for the Student resources. For this open ended URI, only current and directly associated entities are returned. For teachers, this is /sections/{IDs}/studentSectionAssociations/students. {IDs} represents a list of section IDs to which the teacher is currently associated. For staff, this is /schools/{IDs}/studentSchoolAssociations/students. {IDs} represents a list of the school or education organization IDs to which the staff is currently associated.
